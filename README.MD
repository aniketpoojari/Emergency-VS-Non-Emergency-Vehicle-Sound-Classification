# EMERGENCY VS NON EMERGENCY VEHICLE SOUND CLASSIFICATION
- Used Convolutional and LSTM models on audio and spectrogram data for classification of vehicle 
- Achieved training accuracy of 0.87 and validation accuracy of 0.89 on audio data and training accuracy of 0.95 and validation accuracy of 0.94 on spectrogram data for convolutional model. Achieved training accuracy of 0.80 and validation accuracy of 0.81 on audio data and training accuracy of 0.93 and validation accuracy of 0.94 on spectrogram data for LSTM model
- Used: - Python, Keras, librosa, LSTM, CNN
